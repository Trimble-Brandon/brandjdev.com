Overview

Site Purpose

The purpose of my website will be to be a portfolio to display samples of written work. I will have videos that I make for presenting projects I want to post to the site. I love writing and want to be able to have my writing on the website to be viewable and to get valuable feedback. It is a site to follow my written work.

Target Audience

Age range: Early 20s to 40s. Will definitely have the potential to attract all ages. Appeal to women or men: The writing wonâ€™t be specific for men or women, but as a male, the writing is from a male perspective. Specific Country: Any who can speak English, but likely mostly from countries of native English. Area that site visitors live in: Likely more urban. Areas where internet is used more often. Average income/economic class: Want to appeal to all economic classes. Level of education: At least the ability to read. Levels of education will vary. Marital or family status: Will be people of both married and unmarried. Occupation: Other writers, or at least those with an interest in writing. Hours worked per week: Likely more to people who have/take the time to read. Could be full time or less. How often they use the web: Mostly people who use the web more often than not who want to read. What device is used to access the web: Most people seem to use mobile devices these days.

Typography

Arial for the main body text. Arial is nice to read. Lucida Calligraphy for the Large headings. Algerian to provide some interest for the Additional headings. Lucida Calligraphy will also be used for the genres and info in the main page cards.
